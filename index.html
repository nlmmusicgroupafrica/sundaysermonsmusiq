Our Mission

Sunday Sermons MusiQ is a podcast series dedicated to showcasing the authentic sound of electronic music producers. Each Sunday, a different artist takes control of the decks, presenting a mix composed entirely of their own productions. Hosted by I'M MusiQ, this series offers listeners an intimate journey through the creative minds of talented producers, highlighting their unique styles and production techniques.

Our Vision

Sunday Sermons MusiQ was created to provide a platform where electronic music producers can showcase their original work in its purest form. Each episode features a different artist who curates a mix using only their own productions, giving listeners an authentic glimpse into their creative world. We believe in the power of original music and the stories that producers tell through their sound. Join us every Sunday for a new sonic experience that celebrates the art of music production.
